## ADR-009 patches applied (worth preserving for future similar work)

5-point structural critique resolved: (1) asymmetric CHECK on
`parent_doc_id`; (2) partial UNIQUE INDEX guarantees title-lookup
uniqueness; (3) reverse-traversal partial index added; (4) `ON DELETE
RESTRICT` chosen over CASCADE/SET NULL; (5) Rules-parent heuristic
dropped because 부령 제1조 reads "이 규칙은 「○○법」 및 같은 법
시행령에서 위임된 사항…", so single-line title-strip doesn't apply.
